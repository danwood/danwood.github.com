Number Layout

This tutorial does not teach the number row. Most computer keyboards place the numbers in the same order as QWERTY, from 1 to 9 and then 0, going from left to right. The original numeric key layout of the Dvorak keyboar was:

7 5 3 1 9 0 2 4 6 8

(Wikipedia)
